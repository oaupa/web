About Us

  • We are a Congregation of Faith, Family and Friends.

    Our History: In December 2006, God commissioned Pastor Lola Oyeye, her husband Sylvester Oyeye and founded Mountain of Prayer and Praise International Ministries (MOPP) in Houston, Texas. They are devoted parents with two children Samuel and Esther.
